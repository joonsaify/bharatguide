How to Book Driving License Slot in Bhopal – RTO Appointment Guide 2026

📅 Updated: 2026-06-09 · ⏱ 6 min

Booking a driving license slot at Bhopal RTO doesn't have to be confusing. Whether you're applying for a Learner's License (LLR) or the permanent Driving License (DL), here is the exact process for 2026.

Bhopal RTO Offices

Bhopal has two main RTO offices where you can book slots:

OfficeLocationJurisdiction
RTO Bhopal (RTO-47)Govindpura, Near TT NagarBhopal city & rural areas
ARTO BhopalHabibganjSome Bhopal zones

Step 1: Visit the MP Parivahan Website

Go to MP Parivahan PortalOpen https://parivahan.mpg.gov.in or https://sarathi.parivahan.gov.in. The Sarathi portal is the central system used across India.
Select Madhya Pradesh & BhopalChoose Madhya Pradesh as the state and then select Bhopal (RTO-47 or the relevant RTO office) as your RTO.

Step 2: Choose Service Type

You'll see different options. Select what you need:

  • New Learner's License (LLR) — First-time applicant for a learning license
  • New Driving License (DL) — After completing your learner's license period (minimum 30 days for non-transport, 60 days for transport)
  • Renewal — If your DL has expired
  • Duplicate DL — If your license is lost or damaged

Step 3: Fill Application Form

Fill in your personal details: name, date of birth, address, and Aadhaar number. You can save your application number for future reference — note it down.

Step 4: Book Your Slot

Select Date and TimeThe portal shows available dates and time slots for your chosen RTO. Morning slots (10 AM - 1 PM) are usually available. Pick a time that works for you.
Pay the Fee OnlineFees can be paid via debit card, credit card, net banking, or UPI. The fee structure (approximate):
ServiceFee (approx)
Learner's License (LLR)₹150 + test fee
Driving License (DL) Non-Transport₹200 + test fee
DL Transport₹300 + test fee
Renewal (after 5 years)₹200
Get ConfirmationAfter payment, you'll receive a confirmation SMS and email with your slot details. Save this — you'll need it at the RTO.
💡 Tip:Slots at Bhopal RTO fill up fast, especially at the start of the month. Book at least 1-2 weeks in advance during peak seasons (April-June and November-December when students apply).

What to Bring to the RTO

  • Printed application form (from Sarathi portal)
  • Original Aadhaar card + photocopy
  • Address proof (electricity bill, bank statement, etc.)
  • Passport-size photographs (4-6 copies)
  • Medical certificate (Form 1-A) from a registered doctor
  • Fee payment receipt (online payment proof)
  • For DL test: your own vehicle (two-wheeler or car) + helmet

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I book a DL slot without going online?

No, slot booking is fully online through the Sarathi portal. However, you can visit the RTO help desk and they can assist you with the online booking.

What if I miss my slot?

If you miss your appointment, you'll need to book a new slot. The previous fee is generally not refundable. Always reach the RTO at least 30 minutes before your slot time.
