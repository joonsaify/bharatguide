DigiLocker Marksheet Download 2026 – How to Get Class 10, 12 & College Documents

📅 Updated: 2026-06-09 · ⏱ 5 min

DigiLocker has made it incredibly easy to access your academic marksheets and certificates online. Whether you passed Class 10th or 12th this year or graduated years ago, here's exactly how to download your marksheets from DigiLocker.

What is DigiLocker?

DigiLocker is a flagship initiative of the Indian Government under the Digital India program. It provides citizens a secure cloud-based platform to store, share, and verify digital documents. Over 300+ issuing agencies (CBSE, state boards, universities) are integrated.

Step 1: Sign Up for DigiLocker

Visit https://digilocker.gov.in or download the DigiLocker app from Google Play Store.

Register with AadhaarClick 'Sign Up' and enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number. An OTP will be sent to the mobile number registered with Aadhaar. Enter the OTP to verify.
Set a 6-Digit PINAfter OTP verification, create a 6-digit security PIN. This PIN will be required every time you open DigiLocker.
💡 Tip:If your Aadhaar mobile number is not registered, visit the nearest Aadhaar enrollment center to update it. Alternatively, use the 'Register without Aadhaar' option with your mobile number — but Aadhaar-linked accounts get access to more documents.

Step 2: Find Your Marksheet

Go to 'Issued Documents'After logging in, click on 'Issued Documents' section. This shows all documents that government agencies have directly issued to your DigiLocker.
Search for Your Board/UniversityType your board name (e.g., 'CBSE', 'MP Board', 'ICSE') or university name in the search bar. DigiLocker will show available document types.
Select Marksheet DocumentClick on the marksheet or certificate option. You might need to enter your roll number and year of passing for verification.

Step 3: Download or Share

Once the document opens, you can:

  • Click 'Download' to save a PDF copy to your phone/computer
  • Use 'Share' to send a digitally signed copy via email or WhatsApp
  • Use the URI (document ID) for online verification by colleges and employers

Documents Available on DigiLocker for MP Students

  • MP Board (MPBSE) Class 10 Marksheet
  • MP Board (MPBSE) Class 12 Marksheet
  • CBSE Class 10 & 12 Marksheet (all regions including Bhopal)
  • Voter ID (EPIC)
  • Driving License
  • Vehicle RC
  • Ration Card
  • Caste Certificate

Why Use DigiLocker?

DigiLocker documents are legally valid under the IT Act, 2000. They are accepted by most government offices, colleges, and employers. You never need to carry physical copies — just show the DigiLocker app.

💡 Tip:After downloading, save a backup on Google Drive or your computer. While DigiLocker is permanent, having offline access is always helpful during internet outages.

Troubleshooting

  • No documents found? Your board might not have uploaded them yet. Check back after 2-3 weeks of results.
  • Forgot PIN? Use 'Forgot PIN' option on login screen. You'll receive an OTP on your registered mobile.
  • Aadhaar not linking? Ensure your name and DOB match exactly with Aadhaar details.
