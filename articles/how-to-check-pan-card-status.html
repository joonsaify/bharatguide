How to Check PAN Card Status Online 2026 – NSDL & UTIITSL Tracking

📅 Updated: 2026-06-09 · ⏱ 4 min

Applied for a new PAN card or requested a correction? Here is how to check the status of your application on NSDL and UTIITSL portals.

Method 1: NSDL PAN Status Check

Visit NSDL Tracking PageGo to https://tin.tin.nsdl.com/pan/StatusTrack.html. This is the official NSDL PAN tracking page.
Enter Application NumberEnter your 15-digit acknowledgement number. You received this when you submitted your PAN application.
Enter CaptchaType the captcha code and click 'Submit'. Your current status is displayed.

Method 2: UTIITSL PAN Status Check

Visit UTIITSLGo to https://www.utiitsl.com and select 'PAN Services' → 'Track PAN Application'.
Enter DetailsEnter your acknowledgement number and registered mobile number. You'll receive an OTP for verification.
View StatusAfter OTP verification, your complete application status with tracking details is displayed.

Understanding PAN Application Status

StatusMeaningTimeline
Pending / Under ProcessApplication is being verified by NSDL/UTIITSL1-3 working days
AllottedPAN has been generatedImmediate - download e-PAN
DispatchedPhysical card sent via speed post7-10 working days
DeliveredCard delivered (tracking confirmation)Check with your post office
RejectedApplication had errors or incomplete documentsRe-apply with correct details
💡 Tip:Once status shows 'Allotted', you can immediately download e-PAN from NSDL/UTIITSL websites. You don't need to wait for the physical card.
