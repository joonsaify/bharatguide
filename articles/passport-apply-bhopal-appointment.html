Passport Application in Bhopal – How to Book Appointment at PSK & Get Passport Fast

📅 Updated: 2026-06-09 · ⏱ 6 min

Planning to apply for a passport in Bhopal? The process is now mostly online, and if you follow the right steps, you can get your passport in 7-10 working days. Here is the complete guide for 2026.

Bhopal Passport Seva Kendra (PSK)

Bhopal has one Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) that handles all passport applications:

  • Location: Passport Seva Kendra, 1st Floor, A-Wing, Paryavaran Parisar, E-5, Arera Colony, Bhopal - 462016
  • Working Hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Phone: 1800-258-1800 (toll-free)

Step 1: Online Application

Visit Passport Seva WebsiteGo to https://portal2.passportindia.gov.in and register with your email and mobile number.
Fill the Application FormSelect 'Apply for Fresh Passport/Re-issue'. Enter all details carefully — name, DOB, address must match your supporting documents exactly.
Pay the FeeFees are paid online after form submission. Current fee structure:

Book Appointment at Bhopal PSKAfter payment, the portal shows available appointment slots at Bhopal PSK. Choose a date and time. Tatkaal appointments are available separately but have limited slots.
💡 Tip:Normal appointments are available 2-4 weeks ahead. Tatkaal (expedited) appointments can be booked 2-5 days ahead but are more expensive. The Tatkaal quota opens at 9:00 AM daily — book early.

Step 2: Visit PSK with Documents

Required documents checklist:

  • Printed application form (with barcode)
  • Original Aadhaar + photocopy (Proof of Address + ID)
  • Original date of birth proof (Class 10 marksheet / birth certificate / Aadhaar)
  • Address proof (Aadhaar / Voter ID / electricity bill / rent agreement)
  • Passport-size photos (white background, 2"x2") — photos are taken at PSK anyway
  • Existing passport (if re-issue)
  • Marriage certificate (if name changed after marriage)
At the PSKReach 30 minutes before your slot. Process: document verification → photographs → biometrics (fingerprints) → police verification is initiated automatically. The entire process takes 2-3 hours at the PSK.

Step 3: Police Verification

After your PSK visit, local police will visit your residence for verification. In Bhopal, this is usually completed within 5-7 days. Ensure someone is home during working hours. Carry the acknowledgement receipt.

⚠️ Warning:If you submit incorrect address details, police verification will fail and your application will be rejected. Double-check all address details before applying.

Step 4: Passport Dispatch

Once police verification is cleared (usually 3-5 working days after police report submission), your passport is printed and dispatched via Speed Post. Track your application on the Passport Seva portal using your ARN (Application Reference Number).
