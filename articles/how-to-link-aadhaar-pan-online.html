How to Link Aadhaar with PAN Online – Step by Step Guide 2026

📅 Updated: 2026-06-10 · ⏱ 4 min

Linking your Aadhaar with PAN is mandatory for filing income tax returns and for PAN to remain valid. The process takes just 2 minutes online. Here is exactly how to do it.

Why Link Aadhaar with PAN?

As per the Income Tax Act, linking Aadhaar with PAN is mandatory. If you don't link, your PAN becomes inoperative, meaning you cannot file returns, make high-value transactions, or use PAN for financial purposes.

Method: Link on Income Tax e-Filing Portal

Visit Income Tax e-Filing PortalGo to https://www.incometax.gov.in and log in with your PAN and password. New users can register with PAN and Aadhaar.
Go to 'Link Aadhaar' SectionOn the dashboard, click on 'Profile Settings' → 'Link Aadhaar'. Alternatively, use the quick link on the homepage.
Enter Aadhaar NumberEnter your 12-digit Aadhaar number. Your name, DOB, and gender will auto-populate from PAN database. Verify they match your Aadhaar.
Click 'Link Aadhaar'Click the button. An OTP will be sent to your Aadhaar-registered mobile. Enter the OTP to complete linking.
💡 Tip:Use an OTP of ₹10 is charged for this service (via the e-filing portal). SMS charges apply based on your operator. No GST or extra fees.

Alternative: Link via SMS or NSDL

You can also link by sending an SMS to 567678 or 56161 in the format: UIDPAN &lt;12 digit Aadhaar&gt; &lt;10 digit PAN&gt;. Or use the NSDL portal at https://www.tin-nsdl.com/services/pan/link-aadhaar.html.

How to Check if Aadhaar is Already Linked to PAN

Visit Income Tax PortalGo to the e-filing portal and navigate to 'Link Aadhaar Status'. Enter your PAN and Aadhaar to check if already linked.
Alternative: Use Quick LinkOn the Income Tax homepage, use the 'Link Aadhaar Status' quick link. Enter PAN only to see linking status.

Common Issues & Fixes

IssueSolution
Name mismatch between Aadhaar & PANUpdate name on Aadhaar (online at UIDAI) or PAN (via NSDL correction form)
DOB mismatchUpdate DOB on PAN via NSDL correction with proof (birth certificate, school marksheet)
Mobile not linked to AadhaarVisit nearest Aadhaar enrollment center to update mobile number
OTP not receivedWait 5 minutes and try again. If persistent, use SMS method instead

Penalty for Not Linking

If PAN is not linked with Aadhaar, the PAN becomes 'inoperative'. You won't be able to file IT returns, pay taxes online using PAN, or make high-value transactions. There's a late fee of ₹1,000 if linking is done after the deadline.
