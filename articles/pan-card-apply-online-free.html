PAN Card Apply Online Free – Step by Step Guide 2026 (NSDL & UTIITSL)

📅 Updated: 2026-06-09 · ⏱ 6 min

A PAN (Permanent Account Number) card is essential for filing income tax, opening a bank account, making large transactions, and many other financial activities. The good news: applying for a PAN card is completely free online. Here is the exact process for 2026.

Who Needs a PAN Card?

  • Anyone earning taxable income in India
  • Students receiving scholarships (often required)
  • Anyone making transactions above ₹2.5 lakh
  • Anyone opening a Demat account or investing in mutual funds
  • NRIs with Indian financial interests

Two Ways to Apply Online (Both Free)

Option 1: NSDL Portal

Visit NSDL PAN PortalGo to https://www.tin-nsdl.com/services/pan/pan-index.html or directly to the NSDL e-Gov PAN portal.
Select 'Apply New PAN' (Form 49A)Form 49A is for Indian citizens. If you're an NRI, Form 49AA applies. Select the correct form.
Fill Application FormEnter your personal details: name (exactly as on Aadhaar), date of birth, father's name, address, email, and mobile number. Double-check spelling — any mismatch with Aadhaar can cause rejection.
Submit and Get AcknowledgementAfter submission, you'll receive a 15-digit acknowledgement number. Save this — you'll use it to track your application.

Option 2: UTIITSL Portal

Visit UTIITSLGo to https://www.utiitsl.com and select 'PAN Card Services' → 'Apply for New PAN'.
Fill and SubmitThe process is similar to NSDL. UTIITSL also offers doorstep service in many cities.
💡 Tip:Both NSDL and UTIITSL are government-authorized agencies. Neither charges a fee for e-PAN issuance. Physical PAN card delivery has a nominal speed-post charge (₹50-80).

Instant e-PAN: Get PAN in 5 Minutes

If you have Aadhaar, you can get an instant e-PAN completely free. Visit https://www.utiitsl.com → Instant e-PAN. Your Aadhaar-linked mobile will receive an OTP. After verification, your PAN is generated instantly and sent to your registered email.

How to Track PAN Application Status

Go to Tracking PageVisit NSDL or UTIITSL tracking page. On NSDL, it's at https://tin.tin.nsdl.com/pan/StatusTrack.html.
Enter Acknowledgement NumberType your 15-digit acknowledgement number and captcha code.
Check StatusYour status will show as: Under Process → Allotted → Dispatched. Once 'Allotted', you can download e-PAN immediately.

Documents Required

Document TypeAccepted Documents
Proof of Identity (PoI)Aadhaar, Voter ID, Passport, Driving License
Proof of Address (PoA)Aadhaar, Passport, Bank Statement (last 3 months), Electricity Bill
Proof of DOBAadhaar, Birth Certificate, Class 10 Marksheet, Passport
PhotographRecent passport-size photo (digital upload)
💡 Tip:Aadhaar is accepted as both Proof of Identity AND Proof of Address, making it the simplest document. Ensure your Aadhaar address is current.

Important Notes for Bhopal Residents

PAN is processed centrally (not at a local office), so your location doesn't affect processing time. However, if you need a physical PAN card delivered to Bhopal, it typically arrives within 7-15 working days via India Post Speed Post.
