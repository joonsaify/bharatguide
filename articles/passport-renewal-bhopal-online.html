Passport Renewal in Bhopal – Online Re-issue Process 2026

📅 Updated: 2026-06-09 · ⏱ 5 min

Is your passport expired or about to expire? You can renew it easily online. Here is the complete passport renewal (re-issue) process for Bhopal residents.

When to Renew Your Passport

You should apply for passport re-issue in these cases: passport expired, passport expiring within 1 year, no blank pages left for visas, change in personal details (name, address, appearance), damaged passport, or lost passport.

Step-by-Step Renewal Process

Visit Passport Seva PortalGo to https://portal2.passportindia.gov.in and log in with your registered account. If you don't have an account, create one with your email and mobile.
Select 'Re-issue of Passport'Choose 'Re-issue of Passport' as the application type. Select the reason that applies to you (expired, expiring, change in appearance, etc.).
Fill the ApplicationThe form auto-fills many details from your previous passport. Verify and update any changed information, especially address, phone number, and emergency contact.
Pay the FeeFees are paid online. Standard re-issue fee (36-page): ₹1,500. Jumbo (60-page): ₹2,000. Tatkaal: add ₹1,000 extra.
Book Appointment at Bhopal PSKSelect Bhopal Passport Seva Kendra as your PSK. Choose an available date and time. Normal appointments are available 2-3 weeks ahead.
Visit PSK with Old PassportOn the appointment day, carry your old passport, print of the application form, and address proof (if address has changed). Your old passport will be cancelled and returned with a hole punch.

Documents for Renewal

  • Old passport (original + first/last page copy)
  • Passport application form (printed with barcode)
  • Address proof (only if address changed from previous passport)
  • Passport-size photos (white background)
💡 Tip:If your address hasn't changed from your previous passport, you don't need any address proof for renewal. The old passport serves as both identity and address proof.
