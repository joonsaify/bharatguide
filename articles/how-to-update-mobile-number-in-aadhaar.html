How to Update Mobile Number in Aadhaar Online 2026

📅 Updated: 2026-06-09 · ⏱ 5 min

Your Aadhaar-registered mobile number is essential for receiving OTPs, checking status, and using online services. If your number has changed, here is exactly how to update it in 2026.

Why Update Mobile Number in Aadhaar?

An updated mobile number lets you: download e-Aadhaar anytime, receive OTP for online verification, check Aadhaar status via SMS, use mAadhaar app, and link Aadhaar with PAN and bank accounts.

Method 1: Visit Aadhaar Enrollment Center (Recommended)

Find Nearest CenterVisit https://appointments.uidai.gov.in to find your nearest Aadhaar enrollment center. Enter your PIN code and select 'Book Appointment'.
Fill Aadhaar Correction FormDownload Form Aadhaar Correction from UIDAI website. Fill in your details: name, Aadhaar number, old and new mobile number.
Visit with DocumentsTake the filled form, your Aadhaar card copy, and any identity proof to the center. The process takes 10-15 minutes. Biometric verification is required.
💡 Tip:This method is free. The mobile number is updated immediately in the UIDAI database after biometric verification. You'll receive a confirmation SMS on the new number.

Method 2: Self-Service Update Portal

Visit https://ssup.uidai.gov.in. Enter your Aadhaar number and the OTP sent to your registered mobile (if you still have access to the old number). You can request a mobile update, but final verification still requires the enrollment center visit.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card (original or copy)
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar itself is usually sufficient)
  • Correction form (available at center)

What If You Don't Have Access to Old Mobile?

If your old number is no longer active, you must visit the enrollment center with biometric verification. There is no online-only method to change mobile number without access to the old number.
