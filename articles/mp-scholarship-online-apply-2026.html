MP Scholarship 2026 – How to Apply Online for MPPSC, Post-Matric & Pre-Matric Schemes

📅 Updated: 2026-06-09 · ⏱ 5 min

Madhya Pradesh offers several scholarship schemes for students from Class 1 to postgraduate level. If you're a student from Bhopal or anywhere in MP, here's how to apply for scholarships online in 2026.

How to Apply on MP Scholarship Portal

Visit the Official PortalGo to https://scholarshipportal.mp.nic.in (official MP scholarship portal). This is the central portal for all state scholarship schemes.
Register/LoginFirst-time users need to register with their Aadhaar number and mobile. Already registered? Simply log in with your credentials.
Fill Application FormSelect your caste category, scheme type, and enter personal, academic, and bank details. Upload required documents in the specified format (PDF/JPG, under 200KB each).
Verify and SubmitReview all information carefully. Submit the form and save the acknowledgement number.
Institution VerificationAfter submission, your educational institution's head (principal/HOD) must verify your application on the portal. Follow up with your institution after 3-4 days.
💡 Tip:Keep scanned copies of all documents ready before starting: Aadhaar, caste certificate, income certificate (below 2.5 LPA for OBC), bank passbook, previous year marksheet, and passport photo.

How to Apply via National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

Visit NSPGo to https://scholarships.gov.in and register with your mobile number and email.
Search for MP SchemesUse the search to find central schemes applicable to MP students. Apply and submit as above.

Scholarship Deadlines for 2026

Most MP scholarship applications open between July-September after the academic year starts. Late applications are rarely accepted. Set a reminder for mid-July 2026 to check the portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply without an income certificate?

Some schemes require income certificates. Visit your local Tehsil office or apply online on the MP e-District portal (https://edistrict.mp.gov.in) to get one.

What bank account is needed?

A savings account in any nationalized bank with IFSC code. The account should be in the student's name (joint accounts with parents are accepted in some schemes).

How long does verification take?

After submission, institute verification takes 1-2 weeks. District-level verification takes another 2-4 weeks. Scholarship amounts are disbursed in 1-2 installments per academic year.
