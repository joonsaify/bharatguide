How to Download Aadhaar Card Online – e-Aadhaar Download 2026

📅 Updated: 2026-06-09 · ⏱ 4 min

You don't need to wait for the physical Aadhaar letter to arrive by post. You can download e-Aadhaar (digital copy) instantly from the UIDAI website. Here's how.

What is e-Aadhaar?

e-Aadhaar is a digitally signed PDF copy of your Aadhaar card. It is password-protected and legally valid everywhere the physical card is accepted. The password format is: first 4 letters of your name (as on Aadhaar) + year of birth (YYYY).

Method 1: Download by Aadhaar Number

Visit UIDAI WebsiteGo to https://eaadhaar.uidai.gov.in. This is the official e-Aadhaar download portal.
Select 'Download Aadhaar'Click on 'Download Aadhaar' option. Select 'Aadhaar Number' as the input method.
Enter DetailsEnter your 12-digit Aadhaar number, full name (as on Aadhaar), and PIN code. Complete the captcha.
Request OTPClick 'Send OTP'. An OTP will be sent to your Aadhaar-registered mobile number.
Download e-AadhaarEnter the OTP and click 'Download'. A password-protected PDF will be downloaded. Use the password format: first 4 letters of name (CAPITAL) + YYYY birth year.
💡 Tip:Download e-Aadhaar on multiple devices and keep a backup in cloud storage (Google Drive, iCloud). The password-protected PDF is safe to store digitally.

Method 2: Download by Enrollment ID

If you haven't received your Aadhaar yet, use your 14-digit Enrollment ID from the acknowledgement slip. The process is the same as above — select 'Enrolment ID' instead of 'Aadhaar Number'.

What If Your Mobile is Not Registered?

If your mobile is not linked to Aadhaar, you cannot download e-Aadhaar online. Visit the nearest Aadhaar enrollment center to update your mobile number first (see our guide: 'How to Update Mobile Number in Aadhaar').
